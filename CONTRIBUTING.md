# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in **Urban Stack**.

Urban Stack is built on the belief that municipalities achieve more when they collaborate. We welcome contributions from municipalities, public institutions, developers, researchers, and partners who share the vision of open, sovereign, and sustainable digital public infrastructure.

## How You Can Contribute

There are many ways to contribute to the Urban Stack ecosystem:

- 🐛 Report bugs and issues
- 💡 Suggest new features and ideas
- 📖 Improve documentation
- 🧩 Develop new modules and integrations
- 🏛️ Share municipal use cases and best practices
- 🤝 Participate in discussions and community activities

## Before You Start

For significant changes or new features, please create an issue first to discuss your proposal with the community.

This helps ensure alignment with the project vision, roadmap, and architectural principles.

## Our Goal

Urban Stack is more than software.

It is a collaborative effort to build a shared digital foundation for municipalities that promotes:

- Digital Sovereignty
- Open Standards
- Reusability
- Interoperability
- Community-Driven Innovation

Every contribution helps strengthen the ecosystem and benefits municipalities across the public sector.

We appreciate your support and look forward to collaborating with you.
